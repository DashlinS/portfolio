One Piece Gold Rush

This is a Slot Machine with a theme of One Piece! The slot machine allows you $1000 to start and you're able to bet $50, $100, and a custom amount. Anything won through your round can be re-added to the chest to continue playing. Once you are out of money, game over!

This was a really fun project and with the theme of one of my favorite Anime/Manga. I love working with fun projects and will be programming more soon! Check it out and play!
